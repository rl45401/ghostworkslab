FAQ

Workshop notes.

Are these ready to ship?

Some items may be ready-made, while custom pieces are built after the quote and invoice are approved. Product listings will include the current timing.

Can I request custom LED colors?

Custom LED color requests are limited to the top 7 LEDs on the Belt Gizmo and the 16 perimeter LEDs around the static LED on the Frozen Empire-inspired Version 1 Pocket Gizmo.

Do these props include sound or lights?

Available features vary by build. Common options include blinking LEDs, switches, knobs, battery-powered lighting, and other prop-style electronics.

How do PayPal invoices work?

After your request is reviewed, you will receive a PayPal invoice for the approved amount. Work begins once the invoice is paid.

How much is shipping?

A flat shipping rate of $18 is included for all orders. If multiple items are ordered at once, shipping may be combined when possible and reflected in the final quote or invoice.

Are these toys or safety equipment?

No. These are custom fan-made prop electronics for display, cosplay, and collecting. They are not tools, safety devices, or equipment for real paranormal investigation.

Where can I buy available items?

Available ready-made items may be listed on Etsy or eBay. For custom builds, special requests, or anything not currently listed, the custom order form is preferred so all build details can be reviewed in one place.
