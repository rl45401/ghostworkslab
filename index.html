FAQ

Keep this section simple. Answer the questions buyers actually ask before they order.

Are these official Ghostbusters products?

No. These are fan-made custom prop electronics inspired by paranormal gear aesthetics. They are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or licensed by any official franchise entity.

Do you offer custom LED colors?

Yes, depending on the build and current parts availability. Use the custom order request option and include the color you want.

Can I order directly instead of Etsy or eBay?

Yes. Direct orders can be handled through a confirmed custom request followed by a PayPal invoice.

Are these ready to install?

Fully assembled versions are built and tested before shipping. DIY kits require soldering, assembly, and basic electronics comfort.
