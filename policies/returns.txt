Battery and Power Supply Issues
Some GhostWorks Lab products require specific power sources or battery setups.

If a customer chooses to use their own rechargeable USB battery pack, they should be aware that many phone-style power banks include an automatic shutoff feature. Because some prop electronics draw low current, these power banks may shut off during use because they do not detect enough current draw.

For best results, customers should use the included battery pack option when available, a TalentCell-style rechargeable battery pack, or another power source designed for low-current electronics that does not automatically shut off during use.

Modifying TalentCell-style battery packs, rechargeable packs, USB cables, battery packs, or power supplies is discouraged. Battery or power modifications may void warranty coverage and are done entirely at the customer’s own risk.

GhostWorks Lab is not responsible for damage caused by incorrect power supplies, reversed polarity, modified batteries, overvoltage, undervoltage, short circuits, unsafe wiring, or improper battery use.
